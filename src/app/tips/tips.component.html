Tips

 

Static electricity usually occurs when the air humidity is low. Dry and overheated environments build up static, and combing or petting your cat adds the electrical charge.

Humidity zap chance:

  • 0 - 20%: very high zap chance
  • 20 - 30%: high zap chance
  • 30 - 40%: reasonable zap chance
  • 40 - 50%: small zap chance
  • 50 - 100%: you should be safe

Tips to prevent zapping your cat:

  • Put a bowl of water on your heater
  • Buy a humidifier
  • Rub the cat's entire coat with a nylon stocking
  • Add moisture to your cat's fur before combing or petting
  • Do not use plastic combs, metal is better
  • Ground yourself and/or your cat by touching f.e. your heating system
  • Avoid wearing woolen clothes, wear cotton
  • Wear leather-soled shoes, not rubber-soled ones
